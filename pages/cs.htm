Green Technologies in
Computer science engineering & Information technology

Objective:

Green technology is dedicated to fostering energy efficient technology & renewable practices so that the earth’s natural resources are sustained for all life on Earth. The National Conference on “Green Technologies in Computer Science and Engineering and Information Technology” will give you a platform to present your ideas that will help you to grow in technology along with environmental aspects. This time we welcome & present such views that are technically suppose to cope up with the biggest challenge of today’s scenario, i.e. safe green environment.

SCOPE:

  • Green Data Center
  • Parallel Computing
  • Cloud Computing
  • E-Waste Management
  • Grid Computing
  • Green Gadget
  • Wireless Technology
  • Embedded System
  • Green Energy Resources/ Smart Energy Resources
  • Green Computing
  • Green Web
  • Green IT Research
  • Nanotechnology
  • Carbon Footprint
  • Quantum Computing
  • Watermarking
  • Any other relevant topics
