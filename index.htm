AICON-2012 Call for papers
First announcement



Greetings from CSIT It is a matter of great pleasure to introduce ourself as an ISO Certified & NBA Accredited Premier Engineering College of Central India offering excellent technical education. This institute is located on Durg-Balod, Dallirajhara Road at Shivaji Nagar near Kolihapuri, Dist. Durg (C.G.) offers seven under-graduated and three post-graduate programme in Engineering. To show case the development in Science & Technology and provide a platform to researchers CSIT has been organizing All India Conference (AICON) in Mechanical Engineering, Mechatronics Engineering, Computer Science & Engineering, Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering, Electronics & Instrumentation Engineering, Electrical & Electronics Engineering and Information Technology since 2009, which has witnessed tremendous response from academic & Industrial fraternity from all over the country with participations from premier Institute of Country, as well.

The tremendous approach has motivated us to organize AICON-12 with GO GREEN theme but scope not limited to on a much bigger Canvas during 20-21 January 2012. GO GREEN Conference will be an opportunity for researchers/academicians to indulge in deliberation/exchange views and ideas in most hot area of present age.

More information about the conference can be obtained from Institute website csitdurg.in in which conference information page with all necessary details will be uploaded shortly.

Your organization being a state holder in the growth of academic/industrial contribution its expected that your representation in AICON-12 in terms of number and quality will be hall mark.

We also solicit your kind cooperation in circulating, this information to friends, faculty members, research scholars & students and encourage them to participate in this event by submitting paper for consideration at the conference.


Best regards


(Dr. Anurag Verma)
